MarcoPolo

MarcoPolo application is the solution to manage your internet bookmarks. It is simple and easy to use. You can record and find your favorite pages. The bookmarks will be ranked according to your choice. Discover your application, navigate... Enjoy the experience !


Home

This screen allow you to connect to the website or access different information links.

  1. By default, the website MarcoPolo is in French. Click on " French " or " English " to choose the language option.
  2. Enter email address and password in the text fields for identification
  3. A click on " Connect " to access the bookmarks list.
  4. A click on " I want to create an account " Sends to the User account creation page
  5. A click on "I forgot my password ! "Sends the user password on his email box.
  6. A click on "Help" redirects to this current page.
  7. A click on " Who are we ? "Provides informations about MarcoPolo team.
  8. A click on "Contact Us" allows the user to send us a message.

Create an account

This screen allow to create a new user account.

  1. Enter an e-mail adress and a password
  2. Select a language
  3. Click on "Create" to access to your bookmarks library.

Update my profile

This screen allows the user to update his email or unsubscribe from the website.

  1. A click on "Logout" disconnect you from the website.
  2. Enter a new email address to update your profile.
  3. Select your language
  4. A click on "Cancel" directs to the previous page without making any changes.
  5. A click on "Delete" closes the user account.
  6. Click "Edit" to validate the modifications.

Bookmarks List

On this screen the user finds the list of bookmarks. He may add, delete, modify or directly access the internet page of the bookmark.

  1. Click on "My Profile" redirect to the page to update your profile.
  2. Field "Search" can be used to search within the list of names and page links.
  3. Click on "Add" to create a new webpage bookmark.
  4. Click the delete button at the top right of the line to delete the corresponding bookmark.
  5. Click the edit button at the top right of the line to edit the corresponding bookmark.
  6. Click on the bookmark to access to the corresponding webpage.

New bookmark

On this screen the user creates a new bookmark. He must choose a name and a link for it.

  1. Enter a name and a link for your new bookmark
  2. Click on "Create" directs to the edit page of a bookmark after verification mandatory.

Editing Bookmark

This screen allows you to edit a bookmark. You may change the name, the link and also add a tag ( key set value ) in order to describe the bookmark more precisely.

  1. Name and link of your bookmark.
  2. Your bookmark tag list.
  3. Enter a key and a value to create a tag for your bookmark
  4. Click on "+" to create a new tag attached to Submit Thread (both fields are required).
  5. Click "x" to remove a tag.
  6. Click "Cancel" to return to his Submit Thread list.
  7. Click "Delete" to erase a Submit Thread in his intégralitée.
  8. Click "Edit" to validate the change made after verification mandatory.
